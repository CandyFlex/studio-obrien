Our Story

The Ben's Smokehouse Story

Every day starts before sunrise. We light the pit with hickory and oak and let the smoke do the slow work — whole hog, brisket, and ribs cooked low and slow the way it's meant to be.

Every sauce and every side is made from scratch, right here in Spindale. Come hungry, order by the plate or the platter, and find out why folks drive in from all over Rutherford County to stand in our line.

— The Ben's Smokehouse Family

The Method

How We Smoke It

No shortcuts and no microwaves. Just fire, time, and wood smoke.

The pit gets lit at dawn with hickory and oak. Whole hog runs 24 hours, brisket goes 12, and the ribs take 5 or more. Everything else, from the sauces to the sides, is made from scratch that same morning.
