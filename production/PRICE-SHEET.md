# Price Sheet — 3 tracks + the Community pipeline (local-adjusted)

The simple "at the counter" version. Three commercial tracks + one Community
pipeline. Modules (MODULAR-CATALOG.md) roll up into tracks; clients never see
modules. Prices are local-adjusted (see note) — [SET] = Jarred confirms.

> **Local pricing note.** The gathered research (US Website Pricing Research)
> gives NATIONAL figures — freelancer $2-8k, boutique studio $8-15k. Cleveland
> County is a smaller, rural market (the map-pack competitors have 3-12 reviews;
> small owner-operators). So we price WELL BELOW national boutique rates: most
> local builds land $1,500-5,000, monthly $50-500, with the $10k+ scalable
> ceiling reserved for the rare larger client. These anchors reflect that.

## THE THREE TRACKS (what rolls into each)

### ① GET FOUND — the hero (foundation; almost everyone starts here)
*Modules:* GBP management · map-pack tracking + monthly proof report · review
generation · review responses/monitoring · local citations/NAP · local landing
pages. **Care (hosting/security/backups) included, invisible.**
*Delivers:* show up in the map pack + local search, and we prove it monthly.

### ② GET GROWING — the organic engine
*Modules:* on-page + technical SEO · keyword research · AEO · GEO · off-page/links
· blog/articles · lead capture · booking · CRO · analytics.
*Delivers:* rank in search, then capture and convert the traffic.

### ③ GET KNOWN — the reach engine
*Modules:* logo/identity · social & print assets · brand guidelines · social
posting/engagement · Google Ads · Local Services Ads · retargeting · email/sequences.
*Delivers:* look established everywhere + actively reach new customers.

Client-facing choice = **2 to 3 decisions:** start with Get Found, then add
Growing and/or Known. Modules flex inside a track so we pivot without re-quoting.

---

## THE COMMUNITY PIPELINE (core pillar — the enticing launch)

A separate path from commercial. Nominated local businesses get a real Get-Found
build + ongoing growth at a flat community rate. Built to be **very enticing now**
so we land the first 3 case studies fast; **flat, reevaluated later** for new
community rounds.

- **Build: $500 flat** (a real custom Get-Found site — normally $1,500+).
- **Ongoing: $50/mo** — keeps them visible, adds reviews, monthly proof report.
- **The "multiples":** the community package bundles several Get-Found modules
  (GBP + reviews + citations + proof) — generous by design.
- **First 5 = Jarred's marquee picks; then open community nomination** (named
  program). First 3 delivered ASAP = the benchmark case studies.
- Flat rate held for the launch cohort; **reevaluate the number for later rounds.**
