# Sales & Onboarding System — approach → analyze → offer → close → onboard → flywheel

The connective system that turns a spec site or a cold contact into a paying,
reviewing, referring client. It doesn't replace the existing pieces — it wires
them into one journey:
- **OFFERINGS.md** = what we sell (the menu)
- **outreach/OUTREACH-SYSTEM.md** = the approach doctrine (give-before-asking)
- **BUSINESS-INTAKE.md** = the research→build intake for spec sites
- **website-cost-calculator.html** = the pricing engine (studio route)
- **POSITIONING.md** = the voice for every word
This doc adds the missing middle: the standardized ANALYSIS, the PACKAGING +
PRICE POINTS, the PROPOSAL, and the CLIENT ONBOARDING — and closes the loop
back into Pillar 1 (reviews + local links).

## STAGE 2 — The Local Visibility Audit (the hook, standardized)

The free, true, checkable document we lead with. Uses the tools we now own, so
it's repeatable in ~20 minutes per prospect:
- **GBP:** claimed? complete? right category? reviews vs. the local pack ceiling?
- **Map pack:** where do they rank for "[their service] near me"? (`maps-track.mjs`)
- **Organic + site:** do they appear at all? site speed, mobile, "who owns it"
- **Reviews:** count + rating vs. the competitors above them
- **Competitors:** who's taking the calls right now (LocalIntel / the pack pull)
Output: a one-page plain-language "here's how customers find you today" read —
the OUTREACH Gap Read, now backed by real pull data. The read is the give; the
call is optional; the pitch only happens if their reaction invites it.
→ TO BUILD: `AUDIT-TEMPLATE.md` + the exact tool commands that fill it.

## STAGE 3 — Packaging & price points (the core gap this fills)

Three tiers, built from OFFERINGS, anchored to the calculator's **studio route
($90–165/effective hour)** so the numbers match what the site already tells the
public. Prices below are RECOMMENDED ANCHORS — **Jarred confirms/sets them.**

### Tier 1 — "Get Found"  (Starter)
For a business with no site or a bad DIY one. The essentials done right.
Includes: focused custom 1–3 section site (no template), mobile-first, on-page
SEO, GBP setup/cleanup, contact form, Google Analytics, full ownership.
**Anchor: starting ~$1,500–2,500** (≈20–30 studio hrs).

### Tier 2 — "Get Found & Get Called"  (Growth)  ← the default, most clients
For the local business serious about bringing in customers.
Includes: everything in Starter, plus a full multi-section custom site, brand
basics, local SEO foundation (local pages + citation/NAP consistency + review
strategy setup), and a couple of features (gallery / menu / reviews module).
**Anchor: starting ~$3,000–5,000** (≈35–55 studio hrs).

### Tier 3 — "Local Presence"  (Complete)  — custom
For a business that wants to own its local market.
Includes: everything in Growth, plus full brand & identity, ongoing local SEO,
the review engine run for them, and booking/e-commerce as needed.
**Anchor: custom, typically $5,000+.**

### Add-ons (à la carte, priced from the calculator's feature hours)
- Website rescue & migration — from ~$1,200
- Booking system +18 hrs · E-commerce +42 hrs · Blog/CMS +12 hrs (calculator hrs × rate)
- **Ongoing care** — monthly, sold as "if you want it," never lock-in
  (recommend a simple $75–200/mo band; Jarred sets)

**Pricing-display policy — JARRED DECIDES (open in OFFERINGS too):**

## STAGE 4 — The Proposal (what makes them understand the value)

One page, in their voice, that they can say yes to:
1. **What we found** — 2–3 lines from their audit (their reality, checkable).
2. **What it's costing them** — the gap in plain terms (calls going to the pack
   competitor, invisible on mobile, etc.).
3. **The fix** — the matched tier, itemized scope, what's included, timeline.
4. **See it** — the spec/demo (we already have real ones to show range).
5. **The number** — the quote, written and itemized (calculator-grounded).
6. **One next step** — a call or a yes. Service standards from OFFERINGS.
→ TO BUILD: `PROPOSAL-TEMPLATE.md`.
