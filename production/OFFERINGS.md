## The promise

You run your business. We'll keep it visible. Everything below is just a
different way of delivering that.

## Core offerings

### 1. Custom website (design + build)
**Outcome:** a site that gets you found on Google and turns visitors into
calls, directions, and orders.
Included: strategy call · custom design (no templates) · hand-coded build ·
mobile-first · on-page SEO from day one · Google Analytics · launch checklist ·
you own everything (code, content, domain, accounts).
Timeline: most builds go first call → live in about 14 days. Complex builds
get a written timeline upfront.

### 2. Local SEO + Google Business Profile
**Outcome:** showing up when someone nearby searches for what you sell,
especially in the map results.
Included: GBP setup/cleanup (categories, photos, services, posts cadence) ·
local landing pages where they earn their keep · citation/NAP consistency ·
review strategy (honest one: asking well, never buying) · progress reporting
in plain language.

### 3. Brand & graphic design
**Outcome:** looking as established online as you are in person.
Included: logo + mark · palette and type choices · the practical kit (social
avatars, GBP cover, signage-ready files) · applied consistently across site
and profiles.

### 4. Website rescue & migration
**Outcome:** off Wix/Squarespace/aging WordPress without losing your Google
rankings or your content.
Included: full content migration · redirect map (teach: the part that
preserves your SEO) · speed and accessibility pass · same ownership terms.

### 5. Ongoing care (optional, never required)
**Outcome:** the site stays fast, current, and ranking without you thinking
about it.
Included: updates, edits, monitoring, and a real person answering.
Positioning rule: sold as "if you want it," never as lock-in. The site works
without us; that's the point of ownership.

## Service standards (quote these verbatim)

- You hear back **within one business day**. Jarred reads every message.
- Free estimate, no email wall, no pressure.
- No standing meetings, no phone tag: async updates with recorded walkthroughs.
- When it ships, it's yours: code, content, domain, accounts. Full handoff.
- We'll say "you don't need this" when you don't. Fit matters more than the sale.

## What we don't do

- Template flips or "$500 site by Friday" work: wrong fit, we say so kindly.
- Guaranteed rankings: nobody honest sells those (teach why if asked).
- Fake proof: no bought reviews, no invented testimonials, ever.
