## Priority order (effort vs. value)

### 1. Bing Places — do first (easy win)
bingplaces.com · free · **can import directly from your Google profile** in a
few clicks. Feeds Bing + DuckDuckGo maps. Lowest effort, real coverage.

### 2. Apple Business Connect — Apple Maps (growing, free)
businessconnect.apple.com · claims your spot on Apple Maps / Siri / Spotlight.
Every iPhone user searching "web designer near me" in Maps uses this index.

### 3. Nextdoor — hyperlocal, your actual community
business.nextdoor.com · a free business page. This is Shelby neighbors talking
to each other — arguably the highest word-of-mouth value for a local studio.

### 4. Yelp — trust + a category slot
biz.yelp.com · claim the free listing, add the NAP, description, services,
photos. **Do NOT solicit Yelp reviews** — Yelp's policy is strict and their
filter penalizes prompted reviews (opposite of our Google approach).

### 5. Facebook Business Page — social citation
Confirm one exists and matches the NAP; link it in the GBP social profiles.

### 6. Thumbtack — lead-gen (optional)
thumbtack.com/pro · a services marketplace, not just a citation — it can send
you leads directly, but it's pay-per-lead. Set up the profile; decide later
whether to pay for leads. Lower priority than the free citations above.

# Cleveland County Chamber — citation WITHOUT joining?

**Honest answer:** most chamber member directories are a members-only benefit,
so the standard online listing usually does require paying to join. But you're
right not to assume that's the only path — options, cheapest first:

1. **Just ask.** Call/email the Cleveland County Chamber and ask directly if
   they offer a *free/basic listing*, a *new-business spotlight*, or a
   *community directory* separate from paid membership. Some do. Costs nothing
   to ask, and a 5-min call may get you the citation free.
2. **Free local alternatives** (citations/links, no membership):
   - Uptown Shelby Association · Destination Cleveland County · county
     economic-development / "shop local" directories
   - The Shelby Star (local paper) — a story or business spotlight = a real
     local backlink, stronger than any directory
   - Local Facebook community/business groups
3. **The citation you already own:** the "Site by Studio O'Brien" footer link
   on every client/demo site is a genuine local backlink. Every site you build
   is a citation you control — keep adding it.

**Recommendation:** worth a 5-minute call to ask about a free listing. Don't
pay membership *just* for the citation — membership's real value is
networking/referrals/being the known local business person, which is a separate
decision. For pure SEO citation value, the free alternatives + your client
footer links already do the job.
