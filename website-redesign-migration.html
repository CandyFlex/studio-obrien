Questions about website redesign

How much does a redesign cost?

Every redesign is different. A small business site moving from Wix costs less than a large site with custom functionality. We'll give you a straight quote after reviewing your current site and understanding what you need.

Will I lose my SEO during migration?

No. SEO preservation is built into every migration we do. We map every URL, preserve every meta tag, and set up proper redirects so your rankings don't drop. Google won't even notice you switched — except to see a faster, better site.

How long does a redesign take?

Most redesigns go from first call to live in about 14 days. That includes content migration, SEO preservation, design, and a recorded walkthrough. Larger sites with hundreds of pages may take longer — we'll give you a clear timeline upfront.

Can you match my current branding?

Yes. If you have an established brand — logo, colors, fonts, voice — we'll carry it forward. We can also refresh or evolve your branding if you want a new look without losing your identity.

What platforms do you migrate from?

We migrate from Wix, Squarespace, Weebly, WordPress, GoDaddy, Shopify, and most other platforms. We also handle custom-built sites and outdated HTML sites. If you have a site, we can move it.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes. We offer ongoing support for updates, edits, and changes after launch. Whether you want to tweak something or add a new page, we're here. And since you own the site, you're never locked in.

Ready for a website that doesn't look like 2018?

Tell us what you have now and what you want. We'll tell you exactly what we'd build and how long it'll take. Free. No strings.
