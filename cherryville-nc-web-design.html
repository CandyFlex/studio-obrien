Built for Cherryville and northwestern Gaston County.

Cherryville sits in the northwest corner of Gaston County, a working town built on trucking and trade. This is where Carl Grier Beam started Carolina Freight in 1932, and the C. Grier Beam Truck Museum on Mountain Street still tells that story. It made its name moving freight, and the businesses here run on the same practical, get-it-done spirit.

We build for the people who keep this town going: the contractor and the machine shop, the restaurant on Main Street, the home-service crew covering the county line toward Bessemer City and Shelby. Every site is designed and coded around your business, not stamped out of a template.
