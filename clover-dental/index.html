Founder

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers, before you even have to ask

Don't see yours here? Call, text, or drop by. A human picks up.

Get in Touch
How do I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Call or text the studio at least 48 hours ahead and we'll move you to a slot that works, free of charge.

What happens if I miss my appointment?

Life happens. The first miss is on us; after that, a $50 no-show fee applies to appointments missed without notice, since that chair time could have gone to another patient.

How many cleanings do I actually need per year?

Two, for most people: one every six months. If you're managing gum disease or heavy tartar buildup, your dentist may recommend a third or fourth visit, and we'll explain exactly why.

Do I really need x-rays at my checkup?

We take low-dose digital x-rays once a year because roughly half of what matters (decay between teeth, bone levels, infection) is invisible to the naked eye. If you're pregnant, tell us and we'll coordinate with your OB on timing.

I have a dental emergency. What should I do?

If it's life-threatening (trouble breathing or swallowing, uncontrolled bleeding), call 911. For everything else, call us first. We hold same-day slots for emergencies at every studio.

Do you see kids?

Yes. Clover is built for whole families, and our team is great with first visits. For complex pediatric cases we'll refer you to a trusted children's specialist and coordinate the handoff ourselves.

What services do you offer?

Preventive care, fillings, crowns, bridges, implants, extractions, root canals, whitening, veneers, clear aligners, sedation, and emergency dentistry. If you need something you don't see listed, just ask; odds are we do it.

Do you take my insurance?

We're in network with most major PPO plans. Send us your member info before your visit and we'll verify coverage and estimate your out-of-pocket cost ahead of time.

What if I don't have dental insurance?

The Clover Smile Plan covers your cleanings, exams, and x-rays for one flat monthly price, plus member discounts on treatment. No deductibles, no claim forms, no waiting periods.

Do you offer payment plans or financing?

Yes. We partner with flexible financing providers, many with zero-interest options, and we'll break larger treatment plans into stages that fit your budget.

Can I get a second opinion at Clover?

Absolutely. Bring your existing treatment plan and we'll review it honestly, including telling you when the original plan is exactly right. Second opinions are free for existing patients.

What does my insurance actually cover?

It varies by plan, but preventive visits are usually covered at or near 100%. Before any treatment, we run your benefits and hand you a written estimate of your share before you commit to anything.
