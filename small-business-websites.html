Questions small business owners ask us

How much does a small business website cost?

Every business is different. A retail shop needs different features than a service business or a trade. We'll give you a straight quote after understanding what your business does and what you need the site to accomplish.

How long does it take?

Most small business sites go from first call to live in about 14 days. That includes design, copywriting, and a recorded walkthrough so you can review on your own time.

Do I own my website?

Yes. The domain, hosting account, and every line of code is yours. No lock-in, no monthly fees to us unless you want ongoing support. You're never held hostage by your own website.

I'm not technical — can I still update my site?

Absolutely. We build with tools that let you update text, photos, and hours yourself — no coding required. And if you'd rather not touch it, we handle updates for you.

What if I already have a website?

We'll rebuild it without losing your SEO or your Google reviews. Migration is part of the process — no extra charge, no downtime. Your new site picks up right where your old one left off.

Do you work with very small businesses?

Yes. Solo operations, family businesses, one-person shops — if you're serious about your online presence, we're serious about building it right. The size of your business doesn't determine the quality of your website.

Ready for a website that works as hard as you do?

Tell us about your business. We'll tell you exactly what we'd build and how long it'll take. Free. No strings.
