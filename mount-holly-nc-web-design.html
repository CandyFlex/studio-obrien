Built for Mount Holly and Gaston County.

Mount Holly sits on the Catawba River just twenty minutes west of Charlotte, in the heart of Gaston County's manufacturing sector. The town was named in 1875 after the Mount Holly Cotton Mill, built that year by Abel Peterson Rhyne and his partners. That mill, now being transformed into Muddy River Distillery, tells Mount Holly's story: a working town where real industry happens. Today, American & Efird, the world's second-largest thread maker, runs its corporate offices and six plants here, employing over 980 people in manufacturing that ships worldwide.

We build for the people who run this town: the machine shop and the contractor, the thread manufacturer and the logistics firm, the restaurant downtown and the service crew covering the Catawba Valley. Mount Holly is growing fast, with nearly 30 percent population growth in the last decade. Every site we build is designed and coded around your business, not stamped out of a template.
