Questions restaurant owners ask us

How much does a restaurant website cost?

Every restaurant is different. A steakhouse needs different features than a bakery. We'll give you a straight quote after hearing about your space, your menu, and what you need the site to do.

How long does it take?

Most restaurant sites go from first call to live in about 14 days. That includes menu design, reservation integration, and mobile optimization.

Can you integrate with our reservation system?

Yes. We work with OpenTable, Resy, Tock, and direct booking forms. If you use something else, we'll make it work.

Will my menu be easy to update?

Yes. We build with tools that let you change items, prices, and specials yourself, no coding, no calling us every time.

Do you handle online ordering?

Yes. We integrate with Toast, Square, and direct platforms so you keep more revenue instead of losing it to third-party delivery fees.

What if we already have a website?

We'll rebuild it without losing your SEO or your Google reviews. Migration is part of the process: no extra charge, no downtime.
