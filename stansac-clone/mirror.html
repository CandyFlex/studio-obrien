Air Conditioning, Heating, Plumbing, Drains & Electrical Services in Austin, TX

For more than 70 years, Stan’s Heating, Air, Plumbing & Electrical has been Austin’s premier home service company. Our comprehensive care ensures that your property’s most critical systems always operate at peak performance. With licensed and insured experts and 24/7 online booking available, you’ll receive award-winning service backed by a 100% satisfaction guarantee.

HVAC Services

Your home should stay comfortable year-round, and you need professional service to help make that happen. Stan’s is here for all your HVAC service needs, including: 

  • Emergency HVAC repair 
  • HVAC repair 
  • HVAC installation 
  • HVAC replacement 
  • HVAC maintenance 
  • HVAC for new construction 
  • Thermostat installation 
  • Ductwork sealing, repair, and replacement 
  • Air balancing 

Air Conditioning Services

Keep your Austin home cool this summer with AC services from our team. We provide emergency repair for your most urgent issues, along with: 

  • Air conditioning repair 
  • Air conditioning installation 
  • Air conditioning replacement 
  • Air conditioning maintenance 
  • Ductless mini-split services 

Heating Services

With heating services from Stan’s, you can keep your home warm and cozy all winter and are just a phone call away if you need an emergency repair. We have the expertise to provide care for a full range of heating systems, including: 

  • Heating repair 
  • Heating installation and replacement 
  • Heating maintenance 
  • Furnace services 
  • Heat pump services 
  • Insulation 

Indoor Air Quality Services

The air you breathe every day should be fresh and clean. That’s why we offer indoor air quality solutions that support a healthy environment, including:  

  • UV light installation 
  • Air purifier installation 
  • Dehumidifier installation 

Expert Plumbing Services

Our team of professional plumbers offers emergency repairs for issues like burst pipes and no hot water. Our comprehensive plumbing services include: 

  • Water heater repair, replacement, and maintenance 
  • Tankless water heater repair, replacement, and maintenance 
  • Leak detection and repair 
  • Low water pressure repair 
  • Gas line repair and replacement 
  • Water softener installation and repair 
  • Sump pump repair and replacement 
  • Toilet, faucet, and sink repair and replacement 

Drain & Sewer Services

Ensure efficient and clean removal of wastewater from your property with our drain and sewer services. Call today to schedule:  

  • Drain camera inspection 
  • Drain repair 
  • Drain cleaning 
  • Hydro jetting 
  • Sewer line repair and replacement 

Electrical Service

At Stan’s, we prioritize your safety with professional electrical services. From reliable generator performance in a power outage to electrical repairs and replacement, we handle it all, including: 

  • Electrical panel repair and replacement 
  • Whole-home surge protection 
  • Circuit installation 
  • Standby generator installation 
  • EV charger installation 
  • Indoor and outdoor lighting installation 
  • Ceiling fan repair and replacement 
  • Ground wire installation 
  • Outlet and switch installation
