## Owner Background
Don Melton graduated from Winston-Salem Barber School in 1961 and started barbering in 1962 at the former Cleveland Barber Shop. In 1967 he became co-owner of Cantrell & Melton Barber Shop in Uptown Shelby. After his longtime partner and friend retired, Don became sole owner in 2014. Born and raised on his grandfather's farm in Casar, NC, Don still lives there. He also serves on the Cleveland County Water Board of Commissioners (since 2005, chairman since 2007) and owns Don's Pond, a wedding and event venue in Casar.
