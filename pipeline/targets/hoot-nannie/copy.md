## Origin Story

The Hoot Nannie was born from a merger of two existing restaurants. Johnny Ray Bousselot (aka "Ole Johnny Ray") and Stephen Soto (aka "Neighbor Steve") combined the best elements of The Honey Hog (a BBQ/smokehouse in Fallston, NC) and Nannie's Fish Camp & Chicken Champ (Southern fried fare, also in Fallston). They added farm-raised steaks and a full liquor bar, opening The Hoot Nannie on Main Street in Forest City in mid-June 2021, filling a space left vacant after COVID closures (formerly Moe's restaurant).

**The official self-description (from VisitNC/Rutherford County Tourism):**
> The Farmer & Pitmaster combined the best of The Honey Hog and Nannie's Fish & Chicken, added Steak and a full service bar and well, that's The Hoot Nannie! Come see us for the freshest burgers, whole hog BBQ, awesome appetizers and a full liquor bar.

## The Owners

### Johnny Ray Bousselot ("Ole Johnny Ray" / "The Pitmaster")
- Originally from Texas
- Lives in Vale, NC
- Started making hot sauces approximately 12+ years ago under the brand "Johnny Ray's"
- In 2014, began experimenting with a tomato-and-bourbon-based barbecue sauce. Friends and family encouraged him to go commercial.
- Launched **Johnny Ray's Signature Sauces** out of Mount Holly, NC. Now sold in Food Lion and Ingles grocery stores.
- Founded **Johnny Ray's Signature Foods LLC** (Founder/CEO per LinkedIn)
- In 2018, partnered with Tyler Jones (a Shelby native who had run small food markets in Charlotte) to form **Honey Hog Restaurant Group**
- Together they built a farm-and-table operation: they raise their own hogs, Wagyu cattle, and chickens on Honey Hog Farm (126 acres owned, 140 acres leased)
- Manages overall operations across multiple restaurants
- Instagram: @johnnyrays_smokehouse

### Stephen Soto ("Neighbor Steve" / "Executive Chef")
- Lives in Vale, NC
- Runs the day-to-day kitchen operations at The Hoot Nannie
- Executive Chef at Five Spur Steakhouse in Cherryville (the group's newest location)
- Instagram: @neighbor_steve

### Tyler Jones (Partner in Honey Hog Restaurant Group, not active in Hoot Nannie day-to-day)
- Shelby, NC native
- Previously ran three small food markets in the Charlotte area
- In 2015, bought a 40-acre farm in Lincoln County for $4,000/acre
- Sold his grocery markets and partnered with Johnny Ray in 2018

## The Restaurant Group

The Hoot Nannie is part of a growing restaurant group:

1. **Johnny Ray's Smokehouse** (formerly The Honey Hog) - 4629 Fallston Rd, Fallston, NC 28090 (Cleveland County)
2. **Nannie's Fish Camp & Chicken Champ** - Fallston, NC (Cleveland County)
3. **The Hoot Nannie** - 164 E Main St, Forest City, NC 28043 (Rutherford County)
