## Building History

The building at 108 N Lafayette Street was built in 1937 and served as the home of Kester-Groome Furniture Co. for 63 years. When that business closed, the building sat waiting for its next chapter. In 2018, Kellie Ledford and Wren Westbrook brought the space back to life as WorkShop Vintage Market, filling all 6,000 square feet with vendors, vintage finds, and local craftsmanship.
