## Brand Direction Notes

**Color palette suggestion:** Dark, warm, textured. Not neon-punk. Think:
- Rich black (#1a1a1a) as primary
- Warm cream/parchment (#f5f0e6) for contrast
- Deep burgundy or oxblood (#722F37) as accent
- Muted gold (#c4a35a) for warmth
- Charcoal gray for body text

**Mood:** Artisan workshop. Gallery meets studio. The kind of place where oil paintings hang on exposed brick walls and a tattoo machine buzzes in the back room. Serious about craft but welcoming.

**Typography direction:** Strong serif or slab-serif for headings (Playfair Display, Bitter, or similar). Clean sans-serif for body (Inter, DM Sans).

**Visual elements:** Canvas textures, ink marks, brush strokes, warm lighting. Portfolio should be the hero. Large image grids. Let the work do the talking.
