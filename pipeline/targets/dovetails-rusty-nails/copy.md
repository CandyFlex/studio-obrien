### What We Carry Section
**Headline:** Every Corner Holds a Story
**Intro:** From Victorian parlor furniture to vintage baseball cards, our 5,000 square feet of floor space is packed with pieces that have lived real lives. You never know what you will find.

**Categories:**
- **Fine Furniture** - Victorian, Eastlake, shabby chic, primitive. Tables, chairs, dressers, cabinets, and statement pieces spanning more than a century of American craftsmanship.
- **Glass Art & Pottery** - One of the largest collections in the region. Depression glass, art pottery, milk glass, crystal, stoneware, and hand-thrown pieces you will not find anywhere else.
- **China & Tableware** - Complete sets and individual pieces. Bone china, porcelain, sterling flatware, and elegant serving pieces ready for your table or your collection.
- **Vintage Collectibles** - Baseball cards, toys, dolls, records, books, and one-of-a-kind curiosities. The kind of stuff that stops you in your tracks and takes you right back to childhood.
- **Jewelry & Accessories** - Estate jewelry, vintage brooches, watches, cufflinks, and accessories with character that modern pieces simply cannot replicate.

### About Section
**Headline:** Family Roots, Honest Finds
**Body:** Dovetails & Rusty Nails has been a downtown Forest City fixture since 2004. What started as a passion for giving old things new life turned into 5,000 square feet of carefully curated antiques, vintage collectibles, and one-of-a-kind finds.

We are a family-owned shop, and we treat every customer who walks through the door like family too. Whether you are a seasoned collector hunting for a specific piece or just browsing on a Saturday morning, you will find the same warm welcome and honest pricing.

The name says it all. We love the craftsmanship of a hand-cut dovetail joint just as much as the character of a rusty nail in a barn board. Every imperfection tells a story, and every piece in this shop has earned its place.
