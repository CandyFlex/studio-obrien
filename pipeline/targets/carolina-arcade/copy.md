## PBS North Carolina Weekend Feature (Season 21, Episode 21)
Source: pbs.org

Owner Dave Walker and a friend founded the arcade to recreate the social experience of 1980s arcade culture. Rather than requiring tokens or quarters, visitors pay admission and receive a wristband for unlimited play.

The museum houses approximately 105 games on the floor, with another 80 stored at Walker's home, garage, basement, and storage units. Most are classic arcade titles from the 1980s, including Pac-Man and Donkey Kong.

**Owner quote:** "You have more feedback, and more fun when you're playing with somebody, and you're there physically. It's more than just a screen you're looking at."

Though initially targeting 30-50 year olds, the museum attracts families and teenagers seeking a nostalgic, social gaming experience. Opened August 2018 in a converted storefront.
