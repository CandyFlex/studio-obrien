## Description
Garage Gym is a functional fitness gym in Spindale, NC offering group classes, personal training, and open gym access. The gym uses the Wodify platform for class scheduling, membership management, and workout tracking. The name and branding lean into the raw, grassroots spirit of garage-style training. Located in the Foothills of North Carolina, they serve the Spindale, Forest City, and Rutherford County communities.

## Services

### Group Classes
Structured group fitness classes with programmed workouts. Functional movements, high intensity, community-driven atmosphere. Classes are scheduled and booked through the Wodify app.

### Personal Training
One-on-one coaching for members who want individualized programming, movement correction, and focused attention on their goals.

### Open Gym
Access to the facility and equipment outside of class hours. For experienced members who want to train on their own schedule.

- Warm-ups and skill work
- Workout of the Day (WOD)
- Strength cycles
- Metabolic conditioning (MetCons)
- Olympic lifting
- Gymnastics movements (pull-ups, toes-to-bar, muscle-ups)
- Bodyweight and functional movements (burpees, box jumps, wall balls)

## Location Context
Spindale is a small town in Rutherford County, NC, nestled in the foothills west of Charlotte. The area is known for its tight-knit community and outdoor recreation. Garage Gym fills a gap for serious fitness training in a region mostly served by chain gyms like Planet Fitness and Workout Anytime.
