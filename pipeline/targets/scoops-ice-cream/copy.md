## About / Story
Amy and Chris Brevard opened Scoops in 2016 with one shop and a simple idea: serve hand-dipped ice cream that makes people happy. A decade later, four locations stretch across the foothills from Forest City to Marion.

The Forest City shop sits on Broadway Street downtown. The Lake Lure shop catches the vacation crowd. Rutherfordton and Marion round out the footprint. Every location scoops the same way: generous portions, rotating flavors, and staff who actually smile because they want to.
