## About / Concept
Gifts and Talents is a local artisan collective on Main Street in downtown Forest City, NC. The shop brings together makers from across the Foothills region, giving each artisan a place to sell their handcrafted goods under one roof. Rather than a single-owner retail store, it operates as a community marketplace where local talent gets the spotlight.

The name itself tells the story: every item in the shop represents someone's gift, someone's talent. Potters, soap makers, photographers, woodworkers, and furniture restorers all share the same space. Shoppers get variety. Makers get visibility. The community gets a reason to shop local.

Located in the heart of Forest City's walkable downtown, the shop sits among boutiques, restaurants, and small businesses that make up the Main Street district. It is a member of the Rutherford County Chamber of Commerce.

### About
**Headline:** More Than a Shop
**Body:** Gifts and Talents started with a simple idea: give local makers a storefront. Not everyone can run a retail space on their own, but everyone deserves a place to show their work. This collective brings together artisans from Rutherford County and the surrounding Foothills, sharing one space on Forest City's Main Street.

When you shop here, you are buying directly from someone in your community. The person who glazed that mug, carved that bowl, or mixed that soap probably lives a few miles from where you are standing. That connection between maker and buyer is what keeps this place going.
