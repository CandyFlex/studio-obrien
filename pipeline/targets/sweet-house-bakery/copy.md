## About Section Copy
Karren Walls opened Sweet House Bakery in April 2013 with a simple belief: every celebration deserves something baked from scratch, made with real ingredients, and finished with care. What started as a single bakery has grown into three locations, but the heart of the operation remains the same. Every cupcake is frosted by hand, every custom cake is designed with the customer in mind, and every person who walks through the door is greeted with a smile.
