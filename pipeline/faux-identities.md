# Faux Portfolio Businesses

These 10 fictitious businesses exist solely as portfolio showcases. Each demonstrates one archetype design system. They are NOT outreach targets — they show design range.

---

## 1. Quantum Arcade — Retro Neon
- **Industry:** Arcade / Gaming Lounge
- **Location:** Shelby, NC
- **Tagline:** "100+ machines. One wristband. Play all day."
- **Vibe:** Neon-drenched retro arcade with pinball, classic cabinets, and VR stations. Birthday party venue.
- **Archetype:** 01-retro-neon

## 2. Ember & Oak — Industrial Grill
- **Industry:** Gastropub / Craft Burger Bar
- **Location:** Forest City, NC
- **Tagline:** "Wood-fired. Hand-crafted. Locally sourced."
- **Vibe:** Urban gastropub with exposed brick, live-edge wood tables, craft cocktails, and a rotating seasonal menu.
- **Archetype:** 02-industrial-grill

## 3. Black Creek Smokehouse — Rustic Smokehouse
- **Industry:** BBQ / Southern Comfort Food
- **Location:** Shelby, NC
- **Tagline:** "Pit-smoked since 1987. Third generation. Still family."
- **Vibe:** Roadside smokehouse with family recipes, whole-hog BBQ, farm-sourced sides, and sweet tea in mason jars.
- **Archetype:** 03-rustic-smokehouse

## 4. Ninth Circle Studio — Dark Gallery
- **Industry:** Tattoo Studio / Art Gallery
- **Location:** Uptown Shelby, NC
- **Tagline:** "Custom ink. Original art. No flash on the walls."
- **Vibe:** High-end tattoo studio doubling as an art gallery. Black walls, gallery lighting, resident artists with distinct styles.
- **Archetype:** 04-dark-gallery

## 5. Diamond Cut Collectibles — Sports Heritage

- **Location:** Forest City, NC
- **Tagline:** "Hobby shop. Card breaks. Vintage finds."

- **Archetype:** 05-sports-heritage
