Our Story

The Terry Black's BBQ Story

Born and raised in Lockhart, Texas — the barbecue capital of Texas, and perhaps the world — our dad Terry Black passed down generations of barbecue wisdom from his experience in the Lockhart BBQ scene.

We opened Terry Black's to honor that hard-earned family know-how and to bring delicious Central Texas flavors to you with our own unique style.

The High Road on Dawson

Private Events

Perched on a hill overlooking downtown, The "High Road" building is vintage old Austin. This one of a kind property features a large private parking lot, spectacular views and plenty of good times (past and present).

The High Road on Dawson formerly operated as a club and Elks lodge since the 1950's and is now owned and operated by the Terry Black family. The venue is located behind the restaurant at 700 Dawson Road.
