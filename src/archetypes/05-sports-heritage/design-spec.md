# 05 — Sports Heritage

**Vibe:** Vintage card shop, ESPN classic, trophy case, stadium heritage
**Best for:** Sports memorabilia, card shops, collectibles, sports bars, athletic brands

## Color Palette

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| bg | #0f1923 | Main background (deep navy) |
| surface | #1a2a3a | Cards |
| gold | #c9a84c | Primary accent, CTAs, highlights |
| white | #ffffff | Text on dark |
| silver | #a8b4c0 | Secondary text |
| green | #1d5a3a | Field green accent |
| red | #c41e3a | Alternate accent for sports energy |

## Typography

| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Display | Graduate | 400 |
| Headings | Montserrat | 600-700 |
| Body | Inter | 400-500 |

## Patterns & Effects

- **Card layout:** Grid of "trading card" style containers with stats styling
- **Jersey numbers:** Large numeric accents for impact
- **Stripe patterns:** Subtle pinstripe or pennant stripe backgrounds
- **Trophy gold:** Metallic gold gradients on headings and CTAs
- **Stats tables:** Clean data presentation style

## Section Styling

- **Hero:** Navy bg, gold headline, maybe pennant/stripe decorative elements
- **Collection cards:** White-bordered "trading card" layouts with image + stats
- **Buttons:** Gold fill, navy text, slight metallic sheen
- **Dividers:** Thin gold lines

## Component Guidelines
- Use trading card / trophy case design language throughout
- Gold is sacred — use only for primary accents and CTAs
- Typography should feel athletic: bold, clean, impactful
- Cards should have "stats" layout: image top, details below with numeric emphasis
