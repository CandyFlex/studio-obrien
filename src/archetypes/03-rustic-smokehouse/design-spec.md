# 03 — Rustic Smokehouse

**Vibe:** Farm-to-table, Southern hospitality, whole-hog BBQ, family-owned
**Best for:** BBQ joints, steakhouses, farm-to-table restaurants, country diners

## Color Palette

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| bg | #2c1810 | Main background (deep wood brown) |
| surface | #3d2317 | Cards, light sections |
| tan | #d4a574 | Primary accent, borders, highlights |
| cream | #f5f0e8 | Text, light backgrounds |
| burnt | #c1783a | Secondary accent, CTAs |
| green | #4a6741 | Subtle natural accent |
| text | #f5f0e8 | Body text on dark |
| dark-text | #3d2317 | Body text on cream |

## Typography

| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Display | Abril Fatface | 400 |
| Headings | Lora | 500-600 |
| Body | Merriweather | 400 |

## Patterns & Effects

- **Wood grain:** Linear gradient simulating wood texture
- **Burlap texture:** CSS noise or SVG pattern overlay
- **Hand-lettered feel:** Serif display fonts, slightly irregular
- **Warm gradients:** Brown to cream fades
- **Rustic borders:** Rope or twine-inspired divider lines

## Section Styling

- **Hero:** Warm wood bg, serif headline, maybe photo overlay of exterior/food
- **Menu cards:** Cream paper-like surface on wood background, brown borders
- **Buttons:** Burnt orange fill, cream text, slightly rounded
- **Dividers:** Thin brown lines or decorative motif bars

## Component Guidelines
- **PHOTO-FIRST.** Food business. Every menu card must have a real food photo. Never use emojis for food items.
- Menu cards: photo 4:3 top, title + price + description. Image zoom on hover.
- Hero: smoker, interior, or food plate photo with warm brown gradient overlay
- Use serif fonts throughout for a hand-crafted, traditional feel
- Cream/light sections for text-heavy areas, dark wood for imagery
- Keep the palette warm and natural — nothing neon or synthetic
- Photos should feel like they were taken at the farm or in the kitchen
