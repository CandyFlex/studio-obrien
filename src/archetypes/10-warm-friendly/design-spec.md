# 10 — Warm Friendly

**Vibe:** Neighborhood pet store, community shop, local market, family business
**Best for:** Pet stores, groomers, family restaurants, community shops, daycare

## Color Palette

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| bg | #faf5ef | Main background (warm cream) |
| surface | #ffffff | Cards |
| terracotta | #d4956a | Primary accent, CTAs |
| sage | #7a9a7e | Secondary accent, decorative |
| bark | #5c4033 | Text on light bg |
| cream | #fef9ef | Alternate section bg |
| sand | #e8d5c4 | Subtle borders, dividers |

## Typography

| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Display | Baloo 2 | 600-700 |
| Headings | Quicksand | 500-600 |
| Body | Quicksand | 400-500 |

## Patterns & Effects

- **Paw prints:** Subtle paw print motif (CSS shapes or SVG)
- **Rounded everything:** border-radius: 20-28px on all containers
- **Friendly shapes:** Blob-like decorative backgrounds
- **Warm gradients:** Cream to terracotta fades
- **Illustration style:** Playful, approachable visual language

## Section Styling

- **Hero:** Cream bg, large rounded headline, maybe illustrated animal or community imagery
- **Product cards:** White rounded cards with terracotta accents, photo + name + price
- **Service cards:** Sage-accented rounded cards with icon illustrations
- **Buttons:** Terracotta fill, white text, heavily rounded (24px+)

## Component Guidelines
- **PHOTO-FIRST.** Pet stores sell visual products. Product and service cards must use real photos — pets, products, grooming, staff. Never emoji for products.
- Product cards: photo-top layout, product name + price + description
- Warmth and approachability above all else
- Rounded corners on everything — nothing sharp or intimidating
- Terracotta as the primary action color, sage for secondary accents
- Photography should show happy pets, smiling people, real products on shelves
