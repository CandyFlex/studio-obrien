# 07 — Botanical Elegance

**Vibe:** High-end florist, day spa, wedding venue, wellness retreat
**Best for:** Florists, spas, wellness centers, yoga studios, wedding venues

## Color Palette

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| bg | #faf8f5 | Main background (warm white) |
| surface | #ffffff | Cards |
| sage | #8b9d83 | Primary accent, CTAs |
| blush | #d4a0a0 | Secondary accent, decorative |
| cream | #f5efe6 | Alternate section bg |
| text | #3d3535 | Body text |
| muted | #8a8078 | Secondary text |
| gold | #c4a35a | Subtle accent, borders |

## Typography

| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Display | Playfair Display | 400-500 |
| Headings | Cormorant Garamond | 500-600 |
| Body | Lora | 400 |

## Patterns & Effects

- **Watercolor blobs:** Soft, blurred radial gradients as decorative elements
- **Delicate lines:** Thin 1px borders, hairline separators
- **Botanical illustrations:** SVG line art of leaves, petals, vines
- **Soft shadows:** Very subtle box-shadows (2-4px blur, low opacity)
- **Serif-rich:** Editorial, timeless typography

## Section Styling

- **Hero:** Cream bg, elegant serif headline, perhaps watercolor accent shapes
- **Service cards:** White cards with sage accent borders, delicate icon or illustration
- **Gallery:** Large images with generous padding, soft borders
- **Buttons:** Sage fill, white text, slightly rounded, no harsh edges

## Component Guidelines
- Typography is the hero — let serif fonts carry the elegance
- Colors are soft and natural — nothing synthetic
- Whitespace is abundant — breathe between elements
- Botanical illustration accents for decorative touches
- Photography should be light, airy, natural light
