# 01 — Retro Neon

**Vibe:** 80s arcade, synthwave, neon glow, pixel nostalgia
**Best for:** Entertainment venues, arcades, gaming lounges, bowling alleys, retro bars

## Color Palette

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| bg | #0a0a0f | Main background |
| surface | #111118 | Cards, sections |
| pink | #ff2d95 | Primary accent, CTAs, headings |
| cyan | #00e5ff | Secondary accent, links, borders |
| yellow | #ffe53d | Highlights, badges, prices |
| text | #e8e8ec | Body text |
| muted | #8888a0 | Secondary text |

## Typography

| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Display | Press Start 2P | 400 |
| Headings | Orbitron | 500-700 |
| Body | Inter | 400-500 |

## Patterns & Effects

- **Scanline overlay:** repeating-linear-gradient on body at 3px intervals, opacity 0.03
- **Glow:** text-shadow on headings using accent colors (pink on pink, cyan on cyan)
- **Grid background:** dots or squares pattern at low opacity
- **Neon borders:** 1px solid borders with matching box-shadow glow
- **Pixel corners:** clip-path polygon decorations on cards

## Section Styling

- **Hero:** Dark bg with large glowing headline, floating geometric shapes, scanline overlay
- **Cards:** Dark surface bg, neon-accented top border, subtle glow on hover
- **Buttons:** Filled neon accent, dark text, glow shadow on hover
- **Dividers:** Thin neon lines between sections

## Component Guidelines
- **PHOTO-FIRST.** Entertainment venue. Use real arcade interior photography. Hero should be a wide arcade shot with dark overlay.
- Cards have game/machine photos, neon-accented borders
- Use grid backgrounds, scanline overlays, and neon glow effects for atmosphere
- Hover states add intensity to glow effects
- Mobile: reduce glow complexity, keep neon accents visible
- Gallery section showing machines, people playing, and event space
