# 02 — Industrial Grill

**Vibe:** Craft burger joint, whiskey bar, urban gastropub
**Best for:** Restaurants, bars, grills, gastropubs, breweries

## Color Palette

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| bg | #1a1a1a | Main background |
| surface | #242424 | Cards, menus |
| orange | #ff6b35 | Primary accent, CTAs, highlights |
| amber | #f7931e | Secondary accent |
| text | #e8e8e8 | Body text |
| muted | #999999 | Secondary text |
| cream | #f5f0e8 | Light accent for contrast sections |

## Typography

| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Display | Bebas Neue | 400 |
| Headings | Oswald | 500-600 |
| Body | Source Sans 3 | 400-500 |

## Patterns & Effects

- **Grit texture:** CSS noise overlay at low opacity
- **Chalk/menu feel:** Slightly rough, industrial aesthetic
- **Thick borders:** 2-3px solid borders, often orange on dark
- **Staggered layouts:** Asymmetric card placement
- **Condensed type:** Tall, narrow headings for impact

## Section Styling

- **Hero:** Dark bg with warm orange overlay gradient, huge condensed headline
- **Menu cards:** Dark surface, photo on top, orange price tag
- **Buttons:** Solid orange fill, dark text, slight scale on hover
- **Dividers:** Thick single-color bars

## Component Guidelines
- Use heavy, condensed typography for impact
- Orange as the singular pop color — use sparingly for CTAs and accents
- Keep backgrounds consistently dark (charcoal through black)
- Photos should feel warm and appetizing
