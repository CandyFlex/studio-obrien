# 06 — Sweet Nostalgia

**Vibe:** Old-fashioned ice cream parlor, vintage bakery, childhood treats
**Best for:** Bakeries, ice cream shops, candy stores, dessert cafes, cupcake shops

## Color Palette

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| bg | #fff5f5 | Main background (warm white) |
| surface | #ffffff | Cards |
| pink | #ff8fa3 | Primary accent, CTAs |
| mint | #98d8c8 | Secondary accent, decorative |
| vanilla | #f7dc6f | Warm highlight, prices |
| choc | #5c3a21 | Text on light bg |
| cream | #fef9ef | Alternate section bg |

## Typography

| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Display | Fredoka One | 400 |
| Headings | Nunito | 600-700 |
| Body | Nunito | 400-500 |

## Patterns & Effects

- **Drip motifs:** CSS border-radius tricks for ice cream drip effects
- **Sprinkles:** Small colored dots pattern overlay
- **Rounded everything:** border-radius: 16-24px on cards, buttons, images
- **Waffle texture:** Repeating geometric pattern at very low opacity
- **Playful typography:** Rounded, friendly fonts throughout

## Section Styling

- **Hero:** Light cream/pink bg, large friendly headline, maybe illustrated elements
- **Menu cards:** White rounded cards, pink borders, photo + flavor name + price
- **Buttons:** Pink or mint fill, white/choc text, heavily rounded
- **Dividers:** Dotted or dashed pastel lines

## Component Guidelines
- **PHOTO-FIRST.** This archetype is for food businesses. Every product card must have a real photo. Never use icons or emojis to represent food.
- Menu cards: photo 4:3 ratio at top, title + price + description below. Image zoom on hover.
- Hero: full-bleed bakery/product photo with a warm white overlay for text readability
- Photo strip or gallery section between major content blocks to keep the visual appetite going
- Rounded corners everywhere (16-24px minimum)
- Pastels only — no harsh colors
- Photography should show colorful, appetizing products in natural light
- Typography: friendly, rounded, inviting — Fredoka for display, Nunito for body
- Photo credit in footer: "Photography courtesy of Unsplash" for faux sites
