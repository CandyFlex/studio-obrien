Where We Work

01

Shelby Historic District

Shelby, NC  ·  Cleveland County

The heart of Shelby carries more than a century of civic and architectural history. Tree-lined avenues, preserved Victorian and Colonial Revival homes, and a walkable Uptown core make this neighborhood increasingly sought after by buyers who want genuine character rather than new construction imitation.

  • Pre-1950 estate homes on large corner lots
  • Walking distance to Uptown Shelby dining and galleries
  • Active historic preservation community
  • Strong long-term appreciation trajectory
02

Kings Mountain Estates

Kings Mountain, NC  ·  Cleveland County

Perched on the western edge of Cleveland County, Kings Mountain offers executive-level properties with elevated terrain and privacy that is increasingly rare this close to Charlotte. Estate lots, ridgeline views, and quick access to I-85 attract buyers who commute to the city but choose to come home to the foothills.

  • Ridgeline and wooded estate properties
  • 40 minutes to Charlotte Douglas International
  • New custom builds alongside established estates
  • Highly rated Cleveland County Schools
03

Cleveland County Countryside

Rural Cleveland County  ·  NC Foothills

Beyond the town limits, Cleveland County opens into rolling farmland, South Mountains foothills, and creek-fed valleys where acreage still sells at prices the Charlotte market can no longer offer. Buyers seeking equestrian land, gentleman farms, or private retreats find genuine value in this corridor between the piedmont and the mountains.

  • Parcels from 5 to 200+ acres available
  • Equestrian and agricultural properties
  • South Mountains State Park proximity
  • Strong rural land value appreciation
