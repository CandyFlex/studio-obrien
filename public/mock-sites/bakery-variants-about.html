Our Story

Baked with Heart

Karren Walls opened Sweet House Bakery in April 2013 with a simple belief: every celebration deserves something baked from scratch, made with real ingredients, and finished with care.

What started as a single bakery has grown into three locations, but the heart of the operation remains the same. Every cupcake is frosted by hand, every custom cake is designed with the customer in mind, and every person who walks through the door is greeted with a smile.

From six-layer strawberry ombre cakes to a simple box of Red Velvet cupcakes, the goal is always the same: make it fresh, make it beautiful, and make it taste like it was baked just for you.

Our Story

The Sweet House Story

From a single shop to a Shelby staple. Here is how Sweet House Bakery grew.

2013
The Doors Open
Karren Walls opened Sweet House Bakery in Uptown Shelby with a simple mission: bake everything from scratch, use real ingredients, and treat every customer like family.
🎂
2016
Growing the Menu
The cupcake lineup expanded to include signature flavors like Cherry Lemon Sundrop, Cotton Candy, and Salted Caramel. Custom cake orders started pouring in for birthdays and weddings.
2020
Expanding Across the Region
Sweet House Bakery opened its second and third locations, bringing fresh-baked treats to even more communities while keeping the same handmade quality.
🍧
Today
Still Baking with Heart
Three locations, thousands of custom cakes, and a 4.5-star rating. But the approach has not changed. Every cupcake frosted by hand, every customer greeted with a smile.
🍰
