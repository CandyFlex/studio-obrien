Our Story

Built from the Ground Up

Johnny Roy started making barbecue sauces over 12 years ago. That sauce now sits on shelves at Food Lion and Ingles across North Carolina. Good sauce needs good meat. Good meat needs good land.

Good sauce needs good meat. Good meat needs good land.

He partnered with Stephen "Neighbor Steve" Soto, combined the best of The Honey Hog and Nannie's Fish Camp, added farm-raised steaks from Beam Farms, and built a full bar with 68 bourbons. The Hoot Nannie opened June 2021 on Main Street in Forest City.

Behind every plate is Honey Hog Farm. 126 acres owned, 140 leased. Hogs, Wagyu cattle, chickens. All of it raised right here in Rutherford County.
