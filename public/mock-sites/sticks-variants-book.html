What to Know

Common questions about scheduling, deposits, and what to expect.

How do I book an appointment?
Submit a consultation request through the site. Brandon will review your idea and reach out to discuss the piece, placement, sizing, and pricing.
How far out are you booked?
Usually more than a month. Reach out as early as possible, especially for larger or more detailed custom work.
Is a deposit required?
Yes. A non-refundable deposit secures your spot on the schedule. If you need to reschedule due to illness, the deposit transfers to your new appointment date.
Do you accept walk-ins?
Walk-ins are always welcome for flash designs. For custom work, booking in advance is the way to go.
How is pricing determined?
Every piece is different, so every quote is different. Pricing is discussed during your consultation. Brandon provides honest, straightforward estimates.
Why one client per day?
Quality over quantity. One client per day means your session gets Brandon's full, unrushed attention from start to finish.
