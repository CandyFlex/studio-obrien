Always Changing

The Space

6,000 square feet across two floors of curated vintage, handmade goods, antiques, and local art.

Since 2018

What started as a shared vision has grown into a thriving marketplace of pickers, artists, crafters, and collectors.

The Makers

80+ Vendors Under One Roof

WorkShop is a community. Each booth is run by an independent maker, collector, or vintage dealer who brings their own eye, their own taste, and their own passion to the floor. That means the selection is always changing, always growing, and always surprising.

P

Pickers

Treasure hunters who dig through barns, estates, and back roads to find the pieces nobody else can.

A

Artists

Painters, illustrators, and mixed-media creators turning inspiration into one-of-a-kind originals.

C

Crafters

Makers who work with their hands, from handpoured candles and soaps to custom woodwork and textiles.

C

Collectors

Curators of nostalgia, stocking booths with rare vinyl, retro finds, and vintage curiosities.

V

Vintage Dealers

Curated clothing, accessories, and decor with decades of character built right in.

L

Local Makers

Hot sauce, greeting cards, specialty foods, and gifts that capture the spirit of Shelby.
