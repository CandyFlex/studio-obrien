This building has been part of Uptown Shelby for nearly a century. It started as Kester-Groome Furniture Co., which called this address home for 63 years. The showroom floors, the bones of the building, the presence you feel when you step inside. All of that comes from decades of being a place people came to.

When Kellie and Wren opened WorkShop in 2018, they gave the space a second life. Today, 6,000 square feet and two full floors are packed with vendors, vintage finds, and something harder to name. Not nostalgia, exactly. More like proof that the right building, with the right people, keeps going.

Our History

Built in 1937. Reborn in 2018.

1937
The beginning

The building goes up in Uptown Shelby. Kester-Groome Furniture Co. makes it their home, and they will stay for the next six decades. These walls see their first customers, their first displays, their first life.

1937 - 2000
63 years of furniture

For 63 years, Kester-Groome Furniture Co. called this address home. The building became part of Shelby's identity. Generation after generation walked through those doors. The floors wore in. The building settled. History stacked up.

2018
A second life

Kellie and Wren saw what this building could become and opened WorkShop Vintage Market. They kept the bones, honored the history, and filled the space with something new: vendors, vintage finds, and fresh energy running through old walls.

Today
Still going

6,000 square feet. Two full floors. 80+ vendors. And that feeling you get when you walk into a place that has real history in its walls. The building has been here for nearly a century, and it is not done yet.
