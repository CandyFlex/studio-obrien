Brandon Paul, Owner & Artist

The Artist

Brandon Paul

Brandon Paul is a tattoo artist and oil painter with over 14 years of professional experience. After spending years in Colorado building his craft under the Sticks and Stones name, he returned to Shelby to open Uptown's first full-service tattoo shop.

His specialty is creating completely custom pieces of art, both on skin and on canvas. He works in every major style but gravitates toward portraits, detailed black and gray, and vivid full-color work.

Brandon takes one client per day. That is not a marketing line. It is how he works. The result is focused, unrushed, and worth the wait.

Brandon Paul

Tattoo Artist • Oil Painter • 14+ Years

Early Career

Learning the Craft

Brandon picked up a tattoo machine and never looked back. Over the following years, he developed his voice across portraits, black and gray, and full-color work while simultaneously pursuing oil painting.

Colorado

Building the Brand

Operating under the Sticks and Stones name in Bayfield, Colorado, Brandon refined his dual practice. Tattooing by day, painting by night. The name traveled with him.

2019

Coming Home

Brandon returned to Shelby, NC and opened Uptown's first full-service tattoo shop and art gallery. One space for both disciplines, right on Lafayette Street.

Today

One Client, One Day

Brandon takes one client per day. Larger projects that align with his style take priority. He is often booked more than a month out. The result is focused, unrushed, and worth the wait.
