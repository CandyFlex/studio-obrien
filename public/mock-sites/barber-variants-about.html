Don Melton walked into his first barber shop in 1962. Five years later he became co-owner of Cantrell & Melton in Uptown Shelby. That was 1967. The chairs have been filled ever since.

This is not a trendy salon. It is a real barber shop where people come to sit down, catch up, and leave looking sharp. Every barber here takes the craft seriously. And yes, we still use a straight razor.

Our Story

Six Decades of Sharp Cuts

From a young barber's first chair to the cornerstone of Uptown Shelby. Here is how we got here.

1961

Barber School

Don Melton graduated from Winston-Salem Barber School. A small-town kid from Casar, NC with a steady hand and a plan.

1962

First Chair

Don started cutting hair at the former Cleveland Barber Shop. The beginning of a career that would span over sixty years.

1967

Cantrell & Melton Is Born

Don became co-owner and operator of Cantrell & Melton Barber Shop in Uptown Shelby. The chairs have been filled ever since.

2014

Sole Owner

After his longtime partner and friend retired, Don became the sole owner. The tradition continues, same values, same quality.

Today

Still Cutting

Over 60 years later, the shop is still going strong. Voted Best Barber Shop in Cleveland County. Walk-ins welcome, same as always.
