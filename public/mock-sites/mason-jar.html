Our Story

We started with a simple belief: that the food on your table should come from hands you could shake. Every jar on our shelves carries a story. The farmer who grew the tomatoes. The beekeeper who tends the hives three miles up the mountain road. We know them all by name, and we think you should too.

Our shop sits on Main Street in Shelby, right where it has for the last twelve years. Stop in on a Saturday morning and you'll find fresh bread cooling on the counter and coffee from a roaster just down the road.
