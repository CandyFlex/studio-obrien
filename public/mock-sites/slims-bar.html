The Vibe

Not Fancy.

Slim's is what a bar and grill should be. Walk in, grab a seat, order something that actually tastes like someone who cares made it. No pretense, no dress code, no overpriced plates.

Sitting right on East Main Street in downtown Forest City, this is a neighborhood spot through and through. Come for lunch with coworkers, grab dinner with the family, or close out the night with cold ones and good company.

The kitchen runs hot. The beer runs cold. And the burgers run exactly the way you want them to.
