About the Shop

More Than a Shop

Gifts and Talents started with a simple idea: give local makers a storefront. Not everyone can run a retail space on their own, but everyone deserves a place to show their work.

This collective brings together artisans from Rutherford County and the surrounding Foothills, sharing one space on Forest City's Main Street. When you shop here, you are buying directly from someone in your community.

The person who glazed that mug, carved that bowl, or mixed that soap probably lives a few miles from where you are standing. That connection between maker and buyer is what keeps this place going.

How It All Started

From idea to Main Street
1

The Idea

Local makers needed a place to sell their work. Not everyone could afford their own storefront, but the talent was everywhere in the Foothills.

2

The Space

A spot opened up on Main Street in downtown Forest City. The vision was simple: one shared space, many artisans, all local.

3

The Makers

Potters, woodworkers, soap makers, photographers, and furniture restorers came together. Each brought their own craft and their own customers.

4

The Collective

Today, Gifts and Talents is home to over 15 local artisans. Every item on the shelves was made by someone who lives nearby. That is the whole point.
