Our Story

The Farmer & The Pitmaster

Johnny Ray Bousselot and Stephen Soto took the best of two Fallston restaurants, The Honey Hog and Nannie's Fish Camp, added farm-raised steaks and a full bar, and brought it all to Main Street in Forest City.

Behind every plate is Honey Hog Farm. 126 acres of their own land, plus another 140 leased, raising hogs, Wagyu cattle, and chickens. Their steaks come from Beam Farms right here in Rutherford County. The cheese curds come fresh from Brown Creek Creamery.

"You can smell the grill from the parking lot. This is a farm to table type of place. Most of what you eat is sourced locally, and you can taste the difference."

Johnny Ray started making hot sauces over 12 years ago. His signature barbecue sauce is now sold in Food Lion and Ingles stores across the state. That same craft goes into every plate coming out of this kitchen.
