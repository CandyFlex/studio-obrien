What They Raise

Wagyu Cattle Wagyu raised at Honey Hog Farm plus beef from Beam Farms in Rutherford County. This is not commodity beef. Taste the difference.

The Story

🤠
Johnny Ray Bousselot Pitmaster and co-owner. Built The Honey Hog in Fallston on a reputation for whole-hog BBQ and scratch sauces. The smoker came with him to Main Street.
👨‍🍳
Stephen Soto Chef and co-owner. Ran Nannie's Fish Camp in Fallston. When the two operations merged, the menu got a lot more interesting.
🏡
Brought to Main Street The Honey Hog and Nannie's Fish Camp were both Fallston originals. Combined into one place and moved to 164 E Main St, Forest City. The best decision they made.

Farm-Raised Beef Available

Honey Hog Farm raises Wagyu cattle and sources additional beef from Beam Farms in Rutherford County. Ask about availability for cuts to take home. This is about as local as beef gets in Western NC.
