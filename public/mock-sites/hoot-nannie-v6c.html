Where it Comes From

Honey Hog Farm

Johnny Ray and Stephen didn't just open a restaurant. They brought the whole operation with them. 126 owned acres, 140 leased, and animals you'll actually eat two nights later.

The Land
266 Acres, Rutherford County

126 acres owned, 140 leased. Enough room to raise hogs, Wagyu cattle, and chickens the right way. The farm and the restaurant run together on purpose, not as a marketing angle.

The Livestock
Hogs, Wagyu & Chickens

Honey Hog Farm raises the pork you're eating. Beam Farms beef comes from Rutherford County Wagyu. Brown Creek Creamery supplies 150 lbs of cheese curds every week. Local means local here.

Cattle for Sale
Farm-Direct Beef

They sell cattle direct from the farm. Wagyu beef raised on their land, not shipped in from somewhere else. If you want to know where your food comes from, you can literally visit the source.

The Origin Story
Two Restaurants, One Kitchen

The Hoot Nannie is what happens when The Honey Hog and Nannie's Fish Camp from Fallston merge into one spot. Johnny Ray's smoke plus Stephen Soto's kitchen. 164 E Main St is the result.
