Our Story

More Than Just
a Screen

Dave Walker opened Carolina Arcade Museum in August 2018 in a converted storefront right here on Main Street in downtown Forest City.

With over 100 classic arcade and pinball machines, all set to freeplay, the museum recreates the social experience of 1980s arcade culture. No tokens. No quarters. Just pay at the door and play everything.

"You have more feedback, and more fun when you're playing with somebody, and you're there physically. It's more than just a screen you're looking at."

Dave Walker, Founder

What started as a spot for 30-to-50-year-olds reliving their childhood quickly became a destination for families, teenagers, and anyone who wants to put down their phone and play side by side.
