The Artist

Brandon Paul

Brandon Paul is a tattoo artist and oil painter with over 14 years of professional experience. After spending years in Colorado building his craft under the Sticks and Stones name, he returned to Shelby to open Uptown's first full-service tattoo shop.

His specialty is creating completely custom pieces of art, both on skin and on canvas. He works in every major style but gravitates toward portraits, detailed black and gray, and vivid full-color work. Every flash sheet in the shop is hand-drawn. Every custom piece is designed from scratch.

Brandon takes one client per day. That is not a marketing line. It is how he works. Larger projects that align with his style take priority, and he is often booked more than a month out. The result is focused, unrushed, and worth the wait.

Get Started

Book a Session

Every tattoo starts with a conversation. Here is how the process works.

Appointments

All tattoos are done by appointment. Submit a consultation request and Brandon will reach out to discuss your piece, placement, and sizing.

One Client Per Day

Brandon dedicates his full attention to one client each session. This means your piece gets the time and focus it deserves, with no rushing.

Booking Lead Time

The schedule is often filled more than a month out. Reach out early, especially for larger or more detailed custom work.

Deposit

A non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment. If you need to reschedule due to illness, your deposit transfers to the new date.

Walk-ins

Walk-ins are always welcome for flash designs. Stop by and pick a piece from the hand-drawn flash sheets on display in the shop.

Custom Priority

Larger projects that align with Brandon's artistic style take precedence in scheduling. The best pieces come from genuine creative alignment.

Pricing is discussed during consultation. Every piece is unique, so every quote is unique. Reach out with your idea and Brandon will provide an honest estimate.
