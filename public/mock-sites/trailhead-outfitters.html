Our Story

Born on the Trail

Trailhead Outfitters started in a rented storefront on Lafayette Street in 2011, founded by two Cleveland County natives who kept driving two hours to Charlotte to buy decent hiking gear. That felt wrong. The foothills have world-class trails. They should have a world-class shop.

We're not a big-box sporting goods store. We carry fewer products on purpose, and every one of them has been used on local trails by someone on our staff. When you come in, you're talking to people who were at South Mountains last weekend.

We're active in the Cleveland County community: trail maintenance with the Friends of South Mountains, youth outdoor programming through the Foothills Conservancy, and a gear donation program for local Boy Scout and Girl Scout troops. The trail takes care of us. We try to take care of the trail.
