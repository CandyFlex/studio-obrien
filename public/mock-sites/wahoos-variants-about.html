The Shop Story

A Main Street
Treasure Hunt

Wahoo's is a one-man operation on Main Street in downtown Forest City, NC. Owner Bill runs the shop with the hours of someone doing it for the love of it, not the paycheck.

The store started as a sports card and memorabilia shop, rooted in Rutherford County sports heritage and NASCAR country collecting. Over time it evolved to carry sneakers, Supreme streetwear, and vintage sports apparel too.

This is the only shop of its kind in Rutherford County. No pretense, no velvet ropes. Just glass display cases, slatwall fixtures, and cardboard boxes full of finds waiting to be discovered.

How Wahoo's Came to Be

From sports cards to sneakers, one man's passion built the only shop of its kind in Rutherford County.

The Beginning: Cards & Memorabilia

It started as a sports card and memorabilia shop, rooted in Rutherford County sports heritage and NASCAR country collecting. Bill's personal passion for the hobby was the foundation.

The Evolution: Sneakers & Streetwear

Over time, the shop evolved to carry sneakers, Supreme streetwear, and vintage sports apparel. The blend of old-school hobby shop culture with newer hype and resale culture is what makes Wahoo's feel different.

Today: A Main Street Treasure Hunt
