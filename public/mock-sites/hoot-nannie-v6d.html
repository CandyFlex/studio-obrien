Honey Hog Farm

They Raise It.
We Cook It.

Johnny Ray Bousselot runs Honey Hog Farm on 126 owned acres with another 140 leased. Hogs, Wagyu cattle, and chickens. The same animals you read about on the menu are living twenty minutes away.

Beam Farms out of Rutherford County handles their beef program. Brown Creek Creamery supplies 150 pounds of fresh cheese curds every week. When they say local, they mean it in a way most restaurants don't.

The farm also sells beef direct. Wagyu raised right, no middleman.
