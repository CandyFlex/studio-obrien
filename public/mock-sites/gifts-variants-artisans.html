Our Artisans

Stories from the Workbench

Each maker brings a different craft and a different story to the collective.

Ceramics

The Potter

Wheel-thrown ceramics with mountain-inspired glazes. Every piece starts on the wheel and ends in a kiln, shaped by the same hands from start to finish. No two pieces come out the same.

"The clay teaches you patience. You have to let it become what it wants to be."
Woodwork

The Woodworker

Reclaimed hardwoods shaped into cutting boards, bowls, and shelving. The grain tells a story before any stain is applied. Salvaged wood from old barns and fallen trees gets a second life.

"Every board has a history. I just try to honor it."
Wellness

The Soap Maker

Plant-based formulas using essential oils sourced from local farms. Small-batch soaps, balms, and body care products made without shortcuts or synthetic fragrances.

"If I would not use it on my own family, it does not go on the shelf."
Artisans Variant
