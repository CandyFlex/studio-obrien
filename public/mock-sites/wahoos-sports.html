The Shop Story

A Main Street
Treasure Hunt

Wahoo's is a one-man operation on Main Street in downtown Forest City, NC. Owner Bill runs the shop with the hours of someone doing it for the love of it, not the paycheck.

The store started as a sports card and memorabilia shop, rooted in Rutherford County sports heritage and NASCAR country collecting. Over time it evolved to carry sneakers, Supreme streetwear, and vintage sports apparel too. That blend of old-school hobby shop culture with newer hype and resale culture is what makes Wahoo's feel different.

This is the only shop of its kind in Rutherford County. No pretense, no velvet ropes. Just glass display cases, slatwall fixtures, and cardboard boxes full of finds waiting to be discovered.
