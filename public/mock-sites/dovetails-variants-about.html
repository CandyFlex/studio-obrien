Our Story

Family Roots, Honest Finds

Dovetails & Rusty Nails has been a downtown Forest City fixture since 2004. What started as a passion for giving old things new life turned into 5,000 square feet of carefully curated antiques, vintage collectibles, and one-of-a-kind finds.

We are a family-owned shop, and we treat every customer who walks through the door like family too. Whether you are a seasoned collector or just browsing on a Saturday morning, you will find the same warm welcome and honest pricing.

The name says it all. We love the craftsmanship of a hand-cut dovetail joint just as much as the character of a rusty nail in a barn board. Every imperfection tells a story.

Our Story

Family Roots, Honest Finds

Over twenty years of bringing old things back to life on Main Street in Forest City.

2004

Opening Day

What started as a passion for giving old things new life opens its doors at 115 E Main St in downtown Forest City. A family-owned shop from day one.

2008

Growing the Collection

The glass art and pottery collection expands to become one of the largest in the region. Depression glass, art pottery, milk glass, crystal, and stoneware fill the shelves.

2014

A Decade on Main Street

Ten years of family-owned service. The shop grows to 5,000 square feet, packed with Victorian furniture, Eastlake pieces, vintage collectibles, and finds from every era.

2020

Still Standing

Through every challenge, the doors stay open. Customers old and new continue to find treasures, honest prices, and a welcome that feels like visiting family.

Today

20+ Years and Counting

Still family-owned. Still family-run. Still the same warm welcome and honest finds that put us on the map. The name says it all: we love the craftsmanship of a hand-cut dovetail joint just as much as the character of a rusty nail in a barn board.
