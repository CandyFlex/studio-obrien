About the Practice

Rooted in the Foothills

Dr. Sarah Hickory grew up in Cleveland County, graduated from UNC Chapel Hill School of Dentistry, and came straight back home to open this practice in 2011. She wanted to build the kind of office where she would take her own family.

That means an office where the staff knows your name, where nobody rushes you out the door, and where you get honest information about your options without a sales pitch attached. Dr. Hickory is active in the Shelby community, serving on the board of the Cleveland County Health Foundation and volunteering with the Foothills Free Dental Clinic each spring.

Our team has been with us for years. You will see the same faces at every visit, and that consistency matters to us and to our patients.
