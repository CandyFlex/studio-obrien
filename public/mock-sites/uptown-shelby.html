About the Alliance

Built by
This Community

The Uptown Shelby Alliance was formed in 1987 by a group of downtown business owners who believed in the long-term future of their historic district. Cleveland County's courthouse square had seen better days, and they decided to do something about it.

Nearly four decades later, the Alliance coordinates marketing, events, beautification, and advocacy for more than 75 member businesses operating in the heart of Shelby. The work is local, practical, and ongoing.

We are part of the NC Main Street program, recognized statewide for preservation and economic development work. But the pride is Cleveland County's. It always has been.

Our Mission

"To strengthen and promote the economic vitality, historic character, and civic identity of Uptown Shelby, for the benefit of everyone who lives, works, and visits here."
