Who We Are

From Seed
to Cup

Morningside started in 2018 with a sample roaster, a secondhand Probat, and a genuine belief that Shelby deserved good coffee. Not drive-through coffee. Not grocery-store coffee. Coffee with a paper trail, roasted by someone who cares where it came from.

We source green beans direct from cooperatives in Ethiopia, Colombia, Guatemala, and Sumatra. Every lot gets tasted multiple times before it hits the roaster. Roasting happens weekly in small batches so nothing on our shelves is ever stale.

The shop on Warren Street is a community space first. Come in, sit down, and stay as long as you need. We grew up here. This is home.
