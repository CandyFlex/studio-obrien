Our Story

Designer. Baker. Cake whisperer.

Maya Chen left graphic design for pastry school because she wanted to make things people could actually eat. Three years of Charlotte bakeries. One tiny storefront. A pink door. A dream.

She named it Butter + Bloom — butter because everything starts with good butter, bloom because flowers are her inspiration. The croissants started selling out by 9am. The custom cakes started making people cry at weddings. Shelby had found its bakery.
