Our Story

Built for
The Foothills

Iron Peak opened in 2019 with a straightforward idea. Shelby deserved a serious training facility. Not a big-box gym with unused machines, not a boutique studio priced out of reach. A real gym for real people who want to get stronger.

We are part of Cleveland County. Our members are teachers, tradespeople, athletes, and first-timers. The training is hard. The community keeps you coming back. We do not have a secret formula. We just have good coaching, good equipment, and a floor that respects the work.

No Contracts Required Month-to-month memberships. Stay because you want to, not because you have to.
Locally Owned and Operated Every dollar stays in Cleveland County. We are not a franchise.
Coaches Who Actually Coach All 15 of our trainers hold current certifications. They are here to work with you.
