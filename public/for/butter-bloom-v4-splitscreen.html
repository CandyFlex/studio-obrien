Our Story

Designer turned baker.

Maya Chen left graphic design for pastry school in 2016. Three years of Charlotte bakeries taught her craft. A tiny storefront on Lafayette Street gave her a home. Butter + Bloom opened in 2019 — named for her two favorite ingredients.
