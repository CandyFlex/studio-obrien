Our History

Built in 1937. Reborn in 2018.

This building has been part of Uptown Shelby for nearly a century. It started as Kester-Groome Furniture Co., which called this address home for 63 years. When Kellie and Wren opened WorkShop in 2018, they gave the space a second life. Today, 6,000 square feet and two full floors are packed with vendors, vintage finds, and that feeling you get when you walk into a place that has real history in its walls.

1937 The building opens as Kester-Groome Furniture Co. on Lafayette Street.
63 Years Kester-Groome serves Shelby families for over six decades.
2018 Kellie & Wren open WorkShop Vintage Market, giving the building new life.
Today 80+ vendors, 6,000 sq ft, two floors, and a community hub.
