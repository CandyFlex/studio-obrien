Our Story

Beautiful things should taste beautiful, too.

Maya Chen left graphic design for pastry school in 2016. Three years in Charlotte bakeries taught her the craft. A tiny storefront on Lafayette Street gave her a home for it in 2019.

"The croissants sell out by 9am. The custom cakes make people cry at weddings."

Today, Butter + Bloom is Shelby's go-to for celebration cakes and French macarons. Maya still designs every custom cake herself — old habits from the design studio die hard.
