Who We Are

Rooted in the Foothills

Crestview Property Group was founded in Shelby in 2013 by a family with deep roots in Cleveland County. We started with a handful of residential properties and grew by earning the trust of local owners who wanted a firm that would treat their investments as carefully as their own.

We know this market. We know the neighborhoods, the contractors, and the people who make up this community. That local knowledge is something a regional management chain simply cannot replicate, and it shows in our retention numbers.

Today we manage residential and commercial properties across Shelby, Boiling Springs, Kings Mountain, and the surrounding foothills. Every property we take on gets the same direct attention it did when we were just getting started.
