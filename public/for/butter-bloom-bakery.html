Our Story

Beautiful things should taste beautiful, too.

Maya Chen left a career in graphic design to go to pastry school in 2016. She spent three years working in Charlotte bakeries before opening Butter + Bloom in a tiny storefront on Lafayette Street in 2019.

Her philosophy is simple: every cake deserves both a designer's eye and a baker's heart. The name comes from her two favorite ingredients — butter because everything starts with good butter, and bloom because flowers are her design inspiration.

"The croissants sell out by 9am. The custom cakes make people cry at weddings."

Today, Butter + Bloom is Shelby's go-to for custom wedding cakes, delicate French macarons, and butter croissants that rival anything in Charlotte.
