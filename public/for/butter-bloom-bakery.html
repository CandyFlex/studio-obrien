Our Story

Beautiful things should taste beautiful, too.

Maya Chen left a career in graphic design to go to pastry school in 2016. She spent three years working in Charlotte bakeries before opening Butter + Bloom in a tiny storefront on Lafayette Street in 2019.

Her philosophy is simple: every cake deserves both a designer's eye and a baker's heart. The name comes from her two favorite ingredients — "butter" because everything starts with good butter, and "bloom" because flowers are her primary design inspiration.

"The shop quickly became a Uptown Shelby favorite — croissants sell out by 9am."

Today, Butter + Bloom is Shelby's go-to for custom wedding cakes, delicate French macarons in rotating seasonal flavors, and butter croissants that rival anything in Charlotte.
