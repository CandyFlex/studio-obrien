Why I came home

The internet keeps moving faster, and it's leaving a lot of good local businesses behind. Not because they did anything wrong, but because keeping up with Google, AI search, and a website that actually works is now a full-time job on top of the one they already have.

I spent years learning this craft for clients across the country, and I came home to Shelby to be the person who closes that gap for the businesses around me. Studio O'Brien is founder-led and built to grow: the plan is to make it the studio this region reaches for, adding to the team as we go and reinvesting in the area the whole way.

What you walk away with

When your project ships, it is yours. All of it: the code, the content, the domain, the accounts. No platform only we can log into, and no subscription that quietly disables your design the month you stop paying. If you ever move on, you take everything with you.

And the whole process is built to be easy on you. Figuring out what you actually need, then carrying the technical weight so you don't have to, is the part of this work I care about most.
