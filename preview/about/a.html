A letter to the owners of Cleveland County.

From Jarred O'Brien, founder of Studio O'Brien in Shelby, North Carolina.

Jarred O'BrienWeb designer & developer, Shelby NC

The internet keeps moving faster, and it's leaving a lot of good local businesses behind. Not because they did anything wrong, but because keeping up with Google, AI search, and a website that actually works is now a full-time job on top of the one they already have.

I spent years learning this craft for clients across the country. Then I came home to Shelby to point all of it at my own community: to be the person who closes that gap for the businesses around me.

You run your business. I'll keep it visible.

Here's what that means in practice. Every site the studio builds is written in real code, not assembled on a page-builder platform. That keeps it fast, keeps it flexible, and most of all keeps it yours: the code, the content, the domain, all of it. No monthly platform rent, and no template shared with thousands of other companies.

Just as important, I try to make the whole thing easy on you. A lot of owners arrive worn out by past web experiences and unsure what they even need. Figuring that out together, then carrying the technical weight so you don't have to, is the part of this work I care about most.

Studio O'Brien is founder-led and built to grow: the plan is to make it the studio this region reaches for, adding to the team as we go and reinvesting in the area the whole way. If you want to see how I think, I write everything down in The Field Guide, with sources on every number. And the work itself is always the best argument I have.

Jarred O'Brien
Founder, Studio O'Brien · Shelby, NC
