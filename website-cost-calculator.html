What makes our estimates different

Most website cost calculators give you a random number designed to capture your email. Ours is built from 2026 national pricing data and 100+ real projects. More importantly — local competitors charge $3,000-$5,000 for WordPress templates. We deliver custom code at the same price, with copywriting and SEO built in. Here's what drives the numbers.

First 3 pages: $600 each (layout foundation). Pages 4-8: $400 each (content expansion). Pages 9+: $250 each (established architecture). Every page gets custom design, copywriting, and SEO — never templated.

Features

Design Depth

  1. National pricing benchmarks from 2026 industry surveys: freelance custom-coded 5-page sites average $2,500-$5,000; small agencies charge $5,000-$15,000 for the same scope (source: Allusive Digital, JigsawKraft).
