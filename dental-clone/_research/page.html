Frequently Asked Questions

You've got questions, we've got answers

Don’t see the answer you’re looking for?

Get in Touch

How do I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

To cancel or reschedule your appointment we ask that you call or text at least 48 hours before your scheduled appointment time.

What's your appointment cancellation policy?

All cancellations must be made 48 hours in advance.

What if I miss my appointment?

For all missed appointments, without proper notification, a $50 no-show fee will be applied to your account.

How many cleanings do I need per year?

For most patients, we recommend 2 preventative cleaning per year - every 6 months. In some cases, patients may need additional cleanings or a deeper clean like a gum therapy treatment.

Can I opt out of x-rays when I come in for a check up and cleaning?

At Swish, X-rays are needed annually to see what is going on inside the teeth and below the gums to ensure that good oral health is maintained.

I'm pregnant, can I opt out of x-rays when I come in for a check up and cleaning?

At Swish we use low radiation X-rays and according to both the American Dental Association and the American Pregnancy Association, dental X-rays do not have enough radiation to cause any harm to a pregnant woman or her baby. However, we recommend talking to your doctor before and bringing a note from your OBGYN to the visit for clearance and instructions on how to proceed

I have a dental emergency, what should I do?

If it is a true emergency you should call 911 or go to the nearest Emergency Room. For all other dental emergencies you can schedule an emergency appointment online or call the office. Our staff will do everything they can to accommodate you as quickly as possible!

Do you see pediatric patients?

Yes, at Swish we are able to care for your entire family. However, for certain complex pediatric treatments we may refer you to a pediatric specialist.

What services do you offer?

We offer services that fall under General & Family Dentistry, Invisalign, Cosmetic Dentistry, Veneers, Crowns, Bridges, Implants, Facial Aesthetics, Endodontics, Periodontics, Sleep Apnea Treatment, and Oral Surgery. If a service you're interested in isn't listed here, reach out to your nearest studio — chances are we can help!

What if I need a service that is not listed?

If the service you need isn't listed, chances are we still offer it. Our experienced dentists provide a full range of preventative, restorative, cosmetic, and specialty care. Reach out to your nearest Swish studio and we'll let you know what we can do for you.

Do you have specialists in-house?

Yes, we have partnered with the trusted specialists at Complete Specialty Solutions to provide complete oral surgery treatments in Swish owned facilities for your convenience.

Do you take insurance?

We take most PPO plans - use our simple insurance checker to determine if you're covered or call us to discuss what your out of pocket cost may be.

What if I don't have dental insurance?

For most patients, we recommend 2 preventative cleaning per year - every 6 months. In some cases, patients may need additional cleanings or a deeper clean like a gum therapy treatment.

Do you offer payment plans or financing?

Yes, we offer both payment plans and flexible financing through our partners Cherry, HFD, and CareCredit — many with low or no-interest options. You can learn more about our financing options here, or ask your studio team when you book your appointment. We're happy to walk you through what works best for your situation.

What if I want a second opinion?

If another dentist has recommended a treatment plan and you want a second opinion, we are happy to provide one. Just find your nearest Swish studio and book a visit — our team will review your treatment plan and give you an honest, transparent assessment. If you're already a Swish patient and your dentist recommends a procedure, you're welcome to seek a second opinion. We provide in-house second opinions at no additional charge.

If I have a complaint, who can I speak to?

Please call the location and ask to speak to the office manager.

What does my insurance cover?

It depends on your insurer and plan. If you would like to move forward with the treatment plan provided by your dentist we'll run your insurance and give you the best possible estimate of what you'll pay out of pocket.
