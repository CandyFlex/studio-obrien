Questions about SEO

What SEO services do you offer?

On-page SEO, technical SEO, local search strategy, Google Business Profile optimization, keyword research, and content strategy. We focus on getting local businesses found in their specific service area.

How long does SEO take?

Technical fixes and on-page optimization can show results in 2-4 weeks. Competitive keywords take longer. We'll give you realistic timelines upfront — no promises of page one overnight.

Do you do monthly retainers?

We prefer to build the foundation right the first time — clean code, fast load times, proper schema, strong content. After that, if you want ongoing content and monitoring, we can discuss retainer options.

Can you fix my existing site's SEO?

Yes. We audit what's broken, fix the technical issues, and optimize what's already there. No need to rebuild unless the site itself is the problem.

Local vs national SEO?

Local SEO targets searches like "plumber near me" or "restaurant Shelby NC." It focuses on Google Business Profile, local citations, and geo-targeted content. National SEO targets broader terms. Most small businesses only need local.

Do you guarantee rankings?

No. Nobody can honestly guarantee specific rankings — Google's algorithm changes constantly. We guarantee we'll build your site correctly, optimize every page, and track real results transparently.
